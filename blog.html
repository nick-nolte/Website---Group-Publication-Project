Title description, Dec 7, 2017

I believe that we will look back at technology and become shocked with how virtual our lives have become. With the Metaverse developing so quickly, humans will not need to leave their homes to meet new people. The idea is to meet in the Metaverse first and hangout virtually before meeting. Imagine if your friend moves over one thousand miles away, you could ideally still hangout with them in the metaverse. I think it will be hard for our generation to wrap their minds around completely, but the generations that come after us will look at this as normal. The existence of immersive environments will change content creation and practices, because users will now be able to get inspired and learn differently because of what's inside the Metaverse. The metaverse will change the way creators do work in four ways: AI-enabled colleagues, acceleration of learning, immersive team collaboration, and the careers the Metaverse will bring. Some of the mistakes we can avoid when new technology is introduced to our lives is to make sure we are reading what we are clicking. I think in the past we are eager to sign up and be a part of new technology that we are not reading the fine print. I think we should always remember not to be so technology centered and remember there is a physical world still. I think that Facebook and Alphabet can be trusted although they have been proven to be untrustworthy in some litigation suits. Companies make mistakes but I believe that they have come a long way and made enough improvements to keep trust with consumers.
